### Definitions

IAS = Indicated Airspeed
mph = miles per hour (statute)
fpm = feet per minute (mph × 60 min/1 hr )
fps = feet per second (mph × 60 min/1 hr × 60 sec/1 min )
kts = nautical miles per hour, aka knots (fpm × 60 min/1 hr × 1 nm/6076.115 ft)
L/D = Lift ÷ Drag
lbs = Weight in pounds
